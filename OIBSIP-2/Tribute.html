Albert Einstein(1879-1955)

A Short Biography of Albert Einstein:

  • Albert Einstein was born in Germany on 14 March 1879.

  • When he was just five years old, his father bought him a compass. His enquiring mind immediately started trying to work out just why the needle always pointed north!

  • Although he apparently didn’t like school, Einstein showed he had a great mind for maths and science from a young age.

  • In 1900 Einstein graduated with a degree in physics and maths.

  • Rather than becoming a physicist straight away, Albert first trained as a teacher.

  • After failing to find work as a maths and physics teacher, Albert decided to obtain a Ph.D. in physics.

  • One of the discoveries Albert announced in 1905 was his famous formula: E=mc2

  • The formula formed part of Albert’s ‘general theory of relativity’, which he worked on over the next ten years.

  • Albert’s theory of relativity helped scientists understand how the universe works.

  • He went on to win The Nobel Prize in Physics in 1921.
